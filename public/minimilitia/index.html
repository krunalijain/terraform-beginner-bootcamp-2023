Mini Militia, also known as Doodle Army 2

Mini Militia, also known as Doodle Army 2, is an action-packed multiplayer game that has captured the hearts of millions. This addictive game lets you engage in intense battles with friends or players worldwide. With a simple control scheme and a variety of weapons and power-ups, it offers endless fun and excitement. The game's cartoonish graphics and quirky avatars add to its charm. Team up with friends in local or online multiplayer modes to strategize and conquer opponents. Mini Militia is not just a game; it's a platform for camaraderie and competition. Whether you're flying high with a jetpack or engaging in close combat, it keeps you on the edge of your seat. So, gear up, take aim, and jump into the world of Mini Militia for non-stop action and thrilling showdowns.
